Feed Hisashi

In this game you have to catch all the food to feed Hisashi. He is a hungry man on a sakura picnic and does not need cake and candy! He will get upset. You get one point by clicking the food and you will loose a life, whenever you click cake and/or candy. You got 3 lives and 20 seconds. Good luck!
